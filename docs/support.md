# LIRR Ticket Reminder — Support

Thanks for using LIRR Ticket Reminder! If you're experiencing an issue or have a question, you're in the right place.

## Common Questions

**How do I set up a reminder?**
Open the app, choose your station, and configure your preferred alert timing. You'll receive a notification before you board so you never forget to activate your ticket.

**How does the station compass work?**
The compass uses your device's location to show you the nearest LIRR stations and how far away they are. Make sure location permissions are enabled for the best experience.

**Notifications aren't working. What should I do?**
Make sure notifications are enabled for LIRR Ticket Reminder in your device settings: Settings > Notifications > LIRR Ticket Reminder. Also confirm that location permissions are set to "While Using" or "Always."
